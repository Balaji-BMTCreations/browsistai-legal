2. Description of Service

Browsist AI is a comprehensive AI-powered browser assistant providing:

2.1 Core Features

  • AI Chat Assistant: Conversational AI with multiple models (OpenAI GPT-3.5, GPT-4; Google Gemini-2.5)
  • Research Mode (PRO/PREMIUM only): AI chat enhanced with real-time web search, credible sources, and images from the web.   • Voice Input: Speech-to-text capabilities for hands-free interaction
  • Document Analysis: AI-powered analysis of PDFs, Word documents (up to 10MB), and images
  • Multi-Device Sync: Optional cloud synchronization of chat history across devices

  • Browser AI Summarization accessible via AI icon in browser bottom navigation
