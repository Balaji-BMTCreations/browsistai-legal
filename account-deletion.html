How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Browsist AI account using either of these methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1 Open the Browsist AI app on your Android device
2 Navigate to Settings (tap the gear icon in the bottom navigation)
3 Scroll to "Account Actions" section
4 Tap "Delete Account" button
5 Confirm your decision in the first confirmation dialog, then confirm again in the second final confirmation dialog
6 Your account will be marked for deletion and all data will be permanently removed within 30 days

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer email:

1 Send an email to: support@browsistai.com
2 Subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Browsist AI"
3 Include in your email:
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation statement: "I confirm I want to permanently delete my Browsist AI account and all associated data"
4 We will verify your identity and may ask for additional confirmation
5 You will receive a confirmation email within 2-3 business days
6 Your account will be deleted within 30 days of verification

What Happens to Your Subscription

Active Subscriptions:
  • If you have an active PRO or PREMIUM subscription, you must cancel it separately through Google Play Store
  • Account deletion does NOT automatically cancel your subscription
  • To cancel: Open Google Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Browsist AI ‚Üí Cancel
  • You will continue to be charged until you cancel the subscription
  • Refunds are handled by Google Play per their refund policy (typically no refunds for partial months)

Data Retention Period

  • Immediate: Your account is deactivated and you cannot log in
  • Within 7 days: All local device data is cleared (if you're still using the app)
  • Within 30 days: All cloud data (MongoDB Atlas) is permanently deleted
  • After 30 days: Your data is completely removed from our systems and cannot be recovered
  • Backup Systems: Automated backups are also purged within 30 days

Third-Party Data

Please note that data shared with third-party services during your use of Browsist AI is subject to their retention policies:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Retains API data for 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deletes it. See OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: Subject to Google's data retention policies. See Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Payment records retained for 7 years for tax compliance. See Google Payments Privacy
  • Google Sign-In: If you used Google Sign-In, your Google account remains active. Deleting Browsist AI only removes our app's access.

Data Retained After Deletion (Fraud Prevention)

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Disclosure: To prevent abuse of our free tier service, we retain limited information after account deletion:
  • Email address and Google account ID
  • Original account identifier
  • Deletion timestamp
  • Usage statistics at time of deletion (message count, images generated)
  • Subscription status at time of deletion

Purpose: This prevents users from repeatedly creating accounts to exploit free usage limits.

Re-registration: By default, deleted accounts cannot re-register with the same email or Google account.

Your Rights: You may request complete removal of all data, including deletion records, by emailing support@browsistai.com with subject "Complete Data Removal Request". Such requests are reviewed case-by-case within 30 days.

Can I Recover My Account?

‚ö†Ô∏è No Recovery: Once your account is deleted, it CANNOT be recovered. All your data is permanently lost, including:

Re-registration is blocked by default. If you wish to use Browsist AI again, contact support@browsistai.com to request account restoration or re-registration approval.

Alternative: Temporary Account Deactivation

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, consider these alternatives:

  • Disable Cloud Sync: Keep data local-only (Settings ‚Üí Cloud Sync ‚Üí Off)
