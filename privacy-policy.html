Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

Browsist AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name (when you create an account)
  • Authentication Data: Google account information (if using Google Sign-In)
  • Payment Information: Subscription details, payment history (processed securely via Google Play Billing)

1.2 Usage Information

  • Chat History: Your conversations with AI assistants (stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our servers)
  • Browsing Activity: URLs visited within the app, search queries
  • Usage Statistics: Message count, image generation count, feature usage
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and diagnostics for app stability

1.3 Content You Provide

  • Text Input: Messages and prompts sent to AI models
  • Voice Input: Audio recordings for voice-to-text (processed and deleted immediately)
  • Images/Files: Photos and documents uploaded for AI analysis (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Service Delivery: Provide AI chat, image generation, and browser features
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, manage subscriptions
  • Usage Tracking: Monitor quotas, enforce subscription limits
  • Personalization: Improve user experience based on preferences
  • Analytics: Understand feature usage, improve app performance
  • Support: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues
  • Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Information Sharing & Third-Party Services

3.1 AI Service Providers

  • OpenAI: Your messages are sent to OpenAI's GPT models for processing. See OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: Your messages may be processed by Google's AI models. See Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Imagen: Image generation requests processed by Google. Data handling per Google's policies

3.2 Authentication

  • Google Sign-In: If you use Google Sign-In, we receive basic profile information (name, email). See Google Privacy Policy

3.3 Payment Processing

  • Google Play Billing: All subscription payments are processed by Google. We do not store your payment card details. See Google Payments Privacy Notice

3.4 We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Chat history stored on your device using encrypted Room database
  • Cloud Storage: Optional cloud sync via secure MongoDB servers with encryption at rest
  • Data Transmission: All data transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.2+
  • Access Controls: JWT-based authentication, secure session management
  • Data Retention: We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your chat history and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable cloud sync, analytics collection
  • Subscription: Cancel subscription anytime via Google Play

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Chat History: Stored indefinitely (locally) until you delete
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for analytics and debugging
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days

7. Children's Privacy

Browsist AI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States and India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers as required by GDPR and other data protection laws.

9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority

10. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

11. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies:

  • Session Cookies: For authentication (essential)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (optional)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not serve ads

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email to registered users
  • Updated "Last Modified" date
